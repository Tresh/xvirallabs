# XViralLabs — Twitter/X Virality Reverse-Engineering Lab

**XViralLabs** is an AI-powered platform that reverse-engineers why tweets go viral. It analyzes psychology, hooks, platform mechanics, and audience behavior — then helps you generate your own high-probability viral content.

🌐 **Live:** [xvirallabs.com](https://xvirallabs.com)

---

## What It Does

- **10 Analysis Modes** — From viral diagnosis and psychology deconstruction to pattern extraction and engagement forecasting
- **Content Lab** — Full content planning suite with calendars, brand pillars, mind maps, idea banks, and a tweet drafting workspace
- **Memory System** — Store your identity, niche, skills, tone, and custom AI instructions so every generation is personalized
- **Smart Save Routing** — Patterns go to patterns, ideas go to ideas, analyses go to analyses
- **Brand Voice** — Define your writing traits, signature phrases, and words to avoid

## How It Works

1. **Paste a viral tweet** into the analyzer
2. **Pick a mode** — diagnose virality, extract patterns, generate variations, forecast engagement, and more
3. **Save results** to your library — patterns, ideas, and analyses are routed automatically
4. **Use Content Lab** to plan and draft content calendars powered by your saved patterns and brand voice
5. **Set up Memory** so the AI always knows who you are and how you write

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend:** Lovable Cloud (Supabase) — Auth, Database, Edge Functions, Storage
- **AI:** Lovable AI (multi-model) for analysis and content generation
- **Hosting:** Lovable
